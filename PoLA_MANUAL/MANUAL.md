# PoLA - Porosity Local Analysis

Abstract — PoLA (Porosity Local Analysis) is a software tool that describes porous materials (either atomistic models or real samples for which the N$_2$ adsorption isotherm is available) by reporting the “porous character” of all the void points inside the material providing a local, point-by-point description of dimensional and structural properties of the internal void space in three dimensions.

In contrast to conventional approaches that rely on overall pore geometries, PoLA characterizes porosity in terms of the local environment experienced by each point within any void space; hence it is ideal for amorphous and really disordered systems.
